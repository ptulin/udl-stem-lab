Intro to Electric Circuits (Series vs Parallel)

Learn to distinguish series and parallel circuits by current/voltage behavior, predict component failures, and build circuits to meet goals.

Estimated Time: 45-60 minutes

Learning Objectives:
  • Distinguish series vs parallel circuits by current/voltage behavior
  • Predict what happens when a component fails in each circuit
  • Build a circuit configuration to meet a goal (e.g., keep one light on if another fails)
How You'll Be Assessed:

You'll be assessed through interactive circuit building, knowledge checks, and a final challenge.
